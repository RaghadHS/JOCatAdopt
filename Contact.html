ABOUT

Jordan Cat Adoption is a shelter that is concerned with providing a home for stray cats. The shelter was constructed by a group of cat-lovers who hope to spread their noble cause of preserving the lives of helpless creatures to their society.

The shelter is located at the heart of the Jordanian capital, Amman. The shelter's executives hope to open at least one shelter at each Jordanian governorate.

You may pass by our office to adopt a cat directly, or you may fill-in the form in the "Adopt" section. Also, we are very happy to take in cats that don't have a home to stay at our shelter.

For any questions, contact us at info@jocatadopt.com
